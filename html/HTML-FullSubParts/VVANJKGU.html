Subpart 515.6 - Unsolicited Proposals

515.601 Definitions.

“Coordinating office,” as used in this subpart, means:

  1. (a)  The Senior Procurement Executive for all Central Office activities.

  2. (b)  The office designated in writing by the Regional Administrator in the Regions.

515.606 Agency procedures.

Coordinating offices serve as agency points of contact and establish procedures for controlling the receipt, evaluation, and timely disposition of proposals consistent with FAR subpart 15.6.

515.606-2 Evaluation.

Complete the evaluation as soon as practicable, normally within 45 calendar days. Communicate the results of the evaluation to the offeror.
