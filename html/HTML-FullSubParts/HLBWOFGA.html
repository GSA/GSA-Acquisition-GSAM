Subpart 542.12 - Novation and Change-of-Name Agreements

542.1203 Processing agreements.

Contracting officers should process agreements as soon as a complete package has been received from the contractor. If the proposed successor is a large business and the contract was originally awarded under a small business set-aside, the contracting officer shall—

  1. (a) Notify and solicit comments from the SBTA (see FAR 42.1203(b) and (c)); and

  2. (b)  Not recognize the proposed successor if—

    1. (1)  The conclusion is that the transaction is intended to circumvent the requirements and objectives of the small business program; or

    2. (2)  If a MAS contract is involved and other MAS small business contracts exist for the same special item number(s); and

  3. (c)  Cancel the set-aside items if a MAS contract is involved and the contract has both set-aside and non-set-aside special item numbers, then process the novation request for the non-set-aside items.
