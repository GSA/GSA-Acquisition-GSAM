570.106-1 Synopsis of lease awards.

  1. (a)  Except for lease actions described in paragraph (b) of this section, contracting officers must synopsize in http://www.FBO.gov awards exceeding $25,000 total contract value that are likely to result in the award of any subcontracts. However, the dollar threshold is not a prohibition against publicizing an award of a smaller amount when publicizing would be advantageous to industry or to the Government.

  2. (b)  A notice is not required if—

    1. (1)  The notice would disclose the occupant agency’s needs and the disclosure of such needs would compromise the national security; or

    2. (2)  The lease—

      1. (i)  Is for an amount not greater than the simplified lease acquisition threshold;

      2. (ii)  Was made through a means where access to the notice of proposed lease action was provided through http://www.FBO.gov; and

      3. (iii)  Permitted the public to respond to the solicitation electronically.

    3. (3)  Justifications for other than full and open competition must be posted in http://www.FBO.gov. Information exempt from public disclosure must be redacted.
