Subpart 508.4 - Federal Supply Schedules

Parent topic: Part 508 - Required Sources of Supplies And Services

508.404 Use of Federal Supply Schedules.

Although FAR Part 19 (except for the requirement at 19.202-1(e)(1)(iii)) does not apply to BPAs or orders placed against Federal Supply Schedule (FSS) contracts, they are not exempt from part  519.
