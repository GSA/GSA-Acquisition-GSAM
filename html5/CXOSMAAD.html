Subpart 524.2 - Freedom of Information Act

Parent topic: Part 524 - Protection of Privacy and Freedom of Information

524.203 Policy.

  1. (a)  See 41 CFR 105-60 and GSA FOIA procedures available on Insite, for requirements on making records available under FOIA.

  2. (b)  The contracting officer shall notify the appropriate FOIA officer of the request.
